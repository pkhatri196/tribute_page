About

Born in Maharashtra on September 28,1929
Started out singing at religious gatherings with her father
Recorded her first song for a Bollywood film in 1942-she was 13,
Over a 73-year career,she recorded 27,000 songs for Bollywood and regional films
Worked with many legendary Indian music directors:Madan Mohan,Naushad,
SD Burman,RD Burman,Laxmikant-Pyarelal and AR Rahman
Won dozens of singing awards
Awarded the Bharat Ratna,India's highest civilian honour,in 2001.
Legendary singer died at the age of 92.
